Trust & Transparency

With our blockchain-based product, we provide an immutable and transparent ledger where creators can register their original works. The decentralized nature of the blockchain ensures that ownership records are secure, tamper-proof, and easily verifiable, building trust among all parties involved.

02

Virtual Royalty Tracking

Our product automates the royalty tracking process through smart contracts on the blockchain. Every transaction and usage is recorded on the blockchain, enabling accurate and real-time tracking. Smart contracts facilitate automated royalty calculations and distribution.

03

Core Copyright Protection

Our product enhances copyright protection by providing immutable records of ownership on the blockchain. Creators can prove the authenticity and date of creation for their works, strengthening their ability to enforce copyright claims and protect their intellectual property rights.
